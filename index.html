About

SingerJS makes songs on your webpage more beautiful and elegant.

Ideally, the song has a title, an author and a text with chords. Here is a style SingerJS works with:

  • the title (if it exists) is written on the first line
  • the name of author (also optional) is on the second line just after the title
  • the author comes only with the title
  • there is a blank line between the author and text
  • chords are always written on the separate line

Usage


        
Songs are displayed using monospace font in order to keep chords in the same position as they were written, thus it's better to create the song in a text editor which also uses a monospace font (e.g. textEdit in MacOS or Notepad in Windows).


        
Copy the song text inside pre tag:


        
<pre class="singer_song"></pre>


        
Include SingerJS styles in head section of your page:


        
<link href="singerjs.css" rel="stylesheet" />

        


        
Include SingerJS script just before the end of body section:


        
<script src="singer.js"></script>
